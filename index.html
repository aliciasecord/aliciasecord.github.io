About

Alicia Secord is a creative soul living in Detroit. She's spent the last five years making journalism a little more beautiful and exploring the ways design can be used to tell a story. She's dipped her toe into designing for the web and is exploring how her skills can cross over to make the internet a more beautiful place too.

City Center Gallery & Books

Web design and content management

City Center Gallery & Books is a used book store and art gallery in downtown Fayetteville, NC. While working as a store clerk there, I learned HTML and CSS to resdesign their website, in addition to designing monthly email newsletters, managing social media accounts and finding a less expensive website hosting option.

I continued to manage their website for a year after leaving the store position. The website is no longer in use, but an archived copy is available for viewing.

  • Date: May 2010
  • Client: City Center Gallery & Books
  • Category: Website Design, Content Management, Email Newsletters, Social Media Management

Pathfinder Character Generator

In an effort to better understand Javascript, I'm building a character builder for the popular roleplaying game, Pathfinder.

A working version is hosted here or explore the Github repo. Any advice or suggestions is appreciated.
